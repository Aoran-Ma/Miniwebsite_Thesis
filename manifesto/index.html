Queery argues that embracing deviant values and adopting queer design decisions can dismantle hegemonic ideals, fostering unregulated creativity and internal resilience. The central tenet posits that internal failure, often considered a weakness, is the most potent component of queer design acts. By acknowledging and learning from internal failures, individuals can engage in a form of queer design methodology, disrupting normative narratives and contributing to a more inclusive creative landscape.
